### ‚úÖ Core Agreement Structure
- Legally binding agreement language
- Clear acceptance mechanism
- Effective date and last updated date

### ‚úÖ Service Description

  - Not professional advice

### ‚úÖ Eligibility & Accounts
- Age requirement (18+)
- Legal capacity requirements
- Account security responsibilities

### ‚úÖ Subscription & Pricing
- Free tier limitations (30-day retention, rate limits)
- Basic tier benefits ($15/month)
- Billing cycle and payment terms
- Stripe payment processing
- Failed payment handling
- Tax responsibilities

### ‚úÖ Cancellation & Refunds
- Cancellation policy (end of billing period)
- **"All payments are non-refundable"** (with limited exceptions)
- No partial-month refunds
- Downgrade to free tier after cancellation

### ‚úÖ Acceptable Use Policy
- Permitted use (legitimate business purposes)
- Extensive prohibited conduct list:
  - Unauthorized domain analysis
  - Security circumvention
  - Account sharing
  - Reselling/sublicensing
  - Automated scraping
  - Creating competing products
  - Fraud and abuse
- Rate limits and enforcement
- Violation consequences (warning, suspension, termination)

- Company IP ownership (software, designs, content)
- Limited license to users
- User content ownership (domains submitted)

- No warranties (merchantability, fitness, accuracy)

- **AI-generated content** disclaimer (may contain errors)
- Third-party data disclaimer
- **Limitation of liability**:
  - No indirect, consequential, or punitive damages
  - No liability for lost profits, data, or business interruption
  - Cap at $100 or 12-month subscription amount
  - Applies regardless of legal theory

### ‚úÖ Indemnification
- User indemnifies company from:
  - User's violation of Terms
  - User's illegal activity
  - Decisions made based on reports
  - Content submitted by user

### ‚úÖ Dispute Resolution

- Binding arbitration clause
- **Class action waiver** (individual disputes only)
- Small claims court alternative
- Jurisdiction and venue specification
