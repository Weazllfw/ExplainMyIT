## Key Canadian Legal Considerations

### ‚úÖ Advantages of Canadian Jurisdiction

**Consumer Protection**:
- Strong consumer rights under Ontario Consumer Protection Act
- PIPEDA provides robust privacy framework
- Generally business-friendly legal environment

**Arbitration**:
- Arbitration Act (Ontario) supports binding arbitration
- ADR Institute of Canada provides professional arbitration services
- Class action waiver generally enforceable in commercial contracts

**Limitation of Liability**:
- Generally enforceable with proper disclosure
- Consumer Protection Act may limit some waivers, but B2B/B2C distinction matters
- "AS IS" disclaimers recognized in Canadian law

- Requires reasonable security safeguards
- Provides individuals right to access and correct information
