## Key Insights

### What Makes Good Contextual Links
‚úÖ **Natural fit**: Enhances the content, doesn't interrupt flow  
‚úÖ **Adds value**: Gives reader more information on related topic  
‚úÖ **Specific**: Links to exact section (e.g., `/use-cases#insurance-renewal`)  
‚úÖ **Strategic**: Points to money pages, not just other blog posts  

### What to Avoid
‚ùå **Forced links**: Mentioning topics just to add links  
‚ùå **Too many**: More than 3-4 becomes spammy  
‚ùå **Generic**: "Click here" or vague link text  
‚ùå **Redundant**: Multiple links to same page in short span
