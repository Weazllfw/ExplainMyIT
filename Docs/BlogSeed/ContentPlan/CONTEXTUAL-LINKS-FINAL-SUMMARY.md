## Link Quality Standards Used

### ‚úÖ Good Link Placement
- **Natural fit**: Enhances content, doesn't interrupt flow
- **Adds value**: Gives reader more relevant information
- **Specific anchors**: Links to exact sections (e.g., `#insurance-renewal`)
- **Strategic**: Points to high-converting money pages
- **Contextual**: Mentioned when genuinely relevant to discussion

### ‚ùå What We Avoided
- **Forced mentions**: Don't mention topics just to add links
- **Link stuffing**: Never more than 3-4 per article
- **Generic text**: Avoided "click here" or vague anchor text
- **Redundancy**: No multiple links to same page in short span
- **Breaking flow**: Links enhance, never disrupt reading
